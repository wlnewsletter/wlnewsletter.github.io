Visual Analytics Specialist

Adecco Group is looking for a Visual Analytics Specialist with experience in Qlik or Tableau products to work in Budapest for a company that is providing professional services worldwide.

Responsibilities:

  • developing new capabilities for our clients beyond static reports and spreadsheets focusing on Qlik and Tableau products,
  • creating device-agnostic applications that solve specific business problems for desk bound users to mobile field workers,
  • innovating with open technologies such as D3 and HTML5 to create truly unique solutions,
  • creating and delivering end-to-end solutions using new datasources from advancements in sensor and location technologies,
  • developing user interfaces and user experiences that support wide adoption of our solutions.

Requirements:

  • Qlikview & QlikSense or Tableau experience,
  • proven ability to apply analytical and creative thinking,
  • willingness to travel,
  • fluent English.

Advantages:

  • knowledge of any other analytics tool (SAS Visual Analytics, SAP Lumira etc.),
  • HTML5, JavaScript,
  • SQL basics,
  • experience with delivering projects within an agile environment.
